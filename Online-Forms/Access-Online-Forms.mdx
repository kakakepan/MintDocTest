Online Forms is a comprehensive dashboard feature that aids users in efficiently managing various aspects of their domain, such as domain-related contacts and hosting. It empowers resellers to create and personalize domain information tailored to their website. Additionally, resellers can effortlessly oversee website hosting and associated settings through this user-friendly interface.
