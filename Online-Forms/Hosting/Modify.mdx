To **"Modify a hosting"** domain, follow the steps below:

1. Click the **"Online Forms"** button on the home page.

![8](/images/formhostingimages/8.png)

2. Click the **"Hosting"** button.

![9](/images/formhostingimages/9.png)

3. Select **"Modify"** to add a new hosting.

![10](/images/formhostingimages/10.png)

4. Specify the **"Domain"** you want to modify.

![11](/images/formhostingimages/11.png)

5. Specify the **"Command"** to proceed.

![12](/images/formhostingimages/12.png)
