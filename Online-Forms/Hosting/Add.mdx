To **"Add a new hosting"**, follow the steps below:

1. Click the **"Online Forms"** button on the home page.

![1](/images/formhostingimages/1.png)

2. Click the **"Hosting"** button.

![2](/images/formhostingimages/2.png)

3. Select **"Add"** to add a new hosting.

![3](/images/formhostingimages/3.png)

4. Enter the **"Domain name"**.

![4](/images/formhostingimages/4.png)

5. Click **"Submit"** to proceed.

![5](/images/formhostingimages/5.png)

6. Fill in all the required fields.

| Field         | Description                                                |
| :------------ | :--------------------------------------------------------- |
| Hosting Type  | Specify the Hosting type from the available list of types. |
| Hosting Brand | Specify the Hosting Brand of the domain.                   |
| Hosting Plan  | Specify the Hosting plan of the domain.                    |
| SKU           | The unique identifier for the specific hosted domain.      |
| Contact       | The contact for the hosted domain.                         |


![6](/images/formhostingimages/6.png)

7. Click **"Order"** to proceed.

![7](/images/formhostingimages/7.png)
