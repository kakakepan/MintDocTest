# Renew a Hosting

To renew a hosting of a domain, follow the steps below:

1. Click the **Online Forms** button on the home page.

![13](/images/formhostingimages/13.png)

2. Click the **Hosting** button.

![14](/images/formhostingimages/14.png)

3. Select **Renew** to add a new hosting.

![15](/images/formhostingimages/15.png)

4. Select the **domain** you want to renew.
