# Modify Contacts

To modify contacts related to the domain names, follow the steps below:

1. Click the **Online Forms** button on the home page.

![6](/images/formcontactsimages/6.png)

2. Click the **Contacts** button.

![7](/images/formcontactsimages/7.png)

3. Select **Modify** to create a new contact.

![8](/images/formcontactsimages/8.png)

4. Specify the **Contact ** you want to modify.

![9](/images/formcontactsimages/9.png)

5. Specify the **Command**. You can choose to **Update** or **Delete** the Host.

![10](/images/formcontactsimages/10.png)
