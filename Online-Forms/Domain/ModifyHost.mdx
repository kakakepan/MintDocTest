To **"Modify the domain host"**, follow the steps below:

1. Navigate to the homepage and locate the **"Online Forms"** option.
2. Click on the **"Online Forms button"** to proceed.

![38](/images/domainimages/38.png)

3. Select the **"Domains"** button by clicking on it.

![39](/images/domainimages/39.png)

4. Select **"Hosts"** option to proceed.

![40](/images/domainimages/40.png)

5. Click **"Modify"** to start the domain host modification process.

![41](/images/domainimages/41.png)

6. Specify the **"Host"** you want to modify.

![42](/images/domainimages/42.png)

7. Specify the **"Command"**. You can choose to **"Delete"** or **"Modify"** the Host.

![43](/images/domainimages/43.png)
