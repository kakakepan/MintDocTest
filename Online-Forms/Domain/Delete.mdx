# Delete a Domain

To delete a domain, follow the steps below:

1. Navigate to the homepage and locate the **"Online Forms"** option.
2. Click on the **"Online Forms button"** to proceed.

![22](/images/domainimages/22.png)

3. Click on the **"Domains"** by clicking on it.

![23](/images/domainimages/23.png)

4. Select **Delete** option to proceed.

![24](/images/domainimages/24.png)

5. Select the **Domain** you want to delete.

![25](/images/domainimages/25.png)
