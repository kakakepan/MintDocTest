# Add Information

To add additional information to a domain, follow the steps below:

1. Navigate to the homepage and locate the **"Online Forms"** option.
2. Click on the **"Online Forms button"** to proceed.

![26](/images/domainimages/26.png)

3. Click on the **"Domains"** by clicking on it.

![27](/images/domainimages/27.png)

4. Select **Add Information** option to proceed.

![28](/images/domainimages/28.png)
