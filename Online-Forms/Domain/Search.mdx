To utilize the **"Search Domain"** feature, please adhere to the following instructions:

1. Navigate to the homepage and locate the **"Online Forms"** option.
2. Click on the **"Online Forms button"** to proceed.
![1](/images/domainimages/1.png)

3. Select the **"Domains button"** by clicking on it.
![2](/images/domainimages/2.png)

4. Choose the **"Search"** option to proceed.
![3](/images/domainimages/3.png)

5. Enter the **"Domain name"** into the designated field.
![4](/images/domainimages/4.png)

6. Click on the **"Search"** button to initiate the domain search process.
![5](/images/domainimages/5.png)
