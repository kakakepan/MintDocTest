To **"Create the domain host"**, follow the steps below:

1. Navigate to the homepage and locate the **"Online Forms"** option.
2. Click on the **"Online Forms button"** to proceed.

![32](/images/domainimages/32.png)

3. Select the **"Domains"** button by clicking on it.

![33](/images/domainimages/33.png)

4. Select the **"Hosts"** option to proceed.

![34](/images/domainimages/34.png)

5. Click **"Create"** to initiate the domain host creation process.

![35](/images/domainimages/35.png)

6. Specify the **"Host Name"** and the **"Host IP address"**.

![36](/images/domainimages/36.png)

7. Select **"Create"** to confirm the host creation.

![37](/images/domainimages/37.png)
