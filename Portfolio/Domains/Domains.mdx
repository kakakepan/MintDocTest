Within the **"Portfolio"** option, the **"Domains"** section greatly simplifies the management of domains and hosting. This intuitive feature provides valuable insights into the status of domains, including information on whether they are **"active"**, **"inactive"**, **"hosted"**, or **"pending"**, enabling the efficient monitoring and maintenance of domain's online presence with ease.


<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card
    title="Active"
    icon="person-running-fast"
    href="/Portfolio/Domains/Active"
  >
    Specifies the currently active domain that is being used for a website or online service.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Inactive" icon="person" href="/Portfolio/Domains/Inactive">
    Helps users to identify a domain that is currently not in use for a website or online service.
  </Card>
  <Card
    title="Hosts"
    icon="server"
    href="/Portfolio/Domains/Hosts"
  >
    Identifies the location, server and status responsible for the specific hosting provider where the domain's website or email services are hosted.
  </Card>
  <Card
    title="Pending"
    icon="circle-stop"
    href="/Portfolio/Domains/Pending"
  >
    Indicates that there are ongoing processes or actions related to the domain registration or management that have to be resolved before its full functionality becomes available.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
