The **"Hosting"** feature within the portfolio facilitates the management of domain hosting for both inactive and active domains, empowering resellers with hosting services and enabling them to efficiently manage their domain portfolio and its hosting status through a centralized platform. By leveraging this feature, resellers gain the ability to perform the following actions:

  <Card title="Inactive" icon="person" href="/Portfolio/Hosting/Inactive">

</CardGroup>
