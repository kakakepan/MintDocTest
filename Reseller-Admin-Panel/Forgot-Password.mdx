Forgot your account password? Do not fret! The reseller dashboard offers a straightforward password recovery process through its **"Forgot Password"** feature. This feature allows users who have forgotten their passwords to regain access to their accounts.

# Recover Password

To recover your password in the Reseller dashboard, follow the steps below:

1. Navigate to the **Reseller dashboard** [here](https://superreseller-qa.nominus.host/login/).
2. Click the **Forgot Password** button on the Login page to recover your password.

![10](/images/resellerimages/10.png)

3. Enter the **"Email address"** associated with your account.

![11](/images/resellerimages/11.png)

4. Click the **"Reset Password"**. You will receive an email address, follow the instructions on the email address.

![12](/images/resellerimages/12.png)

5. After setting up a new one, you can try to log in to your account [here](https://superreseller-qa.nominus.host/login/).
