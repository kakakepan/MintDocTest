Within the **"Accounting"** option, the **"Hosting Service Prices"** feature  refers to the fees associated with hosting clients' websites, applications, or digital services provided by the **"Register.domains"** . By understanding the hosting service prices, clients can make informed decisions about their hosting needs and choose the most suitable hosting plan offered by the **"Register.domains"**

To view the **"Hosting Service Prices" **for your domain, website, or online service, please follow the steps outlined below:

1. Click on the **"Hosting Service Prices"** feature under the **"Accounting**" option, which provides information such as the following:

| Parameter         | Description                                                                                                                 |
| :---------------- | :-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Hosting Type      | The method or technology used to store and serve website files.                                                             |
| Hosting Brand     | The company or provider that offers the hosting services for the website.                                                   |
| Service           | Indicates the name of the service for which the hosting service order for the domain was placed.                            |
| Price             | Represents the cost or amount associated with the domain hosting service.                                                   |
| Currency          | Indicates the currency in which the price for the domain hosting service is expressed.                                      |
| Min. Time Periods | The minimum duration during which the domain hosting service order can be requested or remains valid.                       |
| Min. Accounts     | Specifies the minimum required user accounts for accessing and managing hosted websites, applications, or digital services. |
