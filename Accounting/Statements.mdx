Within the **"Accounting"** option, the "**Statements"** feature indicates essential financial information that provides a comprehensive summary of all transactions between **"Register.domains"** and the client within a specified time frame. They offer an overview of the services provided during that period, including the corresponding charges for each service. Additionally, "**Statements"** also include information about any payments received from the client and highlight any outstanding balances between  and the client and **"Register.domains"** presenting a clear and concise snapshot of the financial relationship, statements facilitate transparency, help in reconciling accounts, and assist in managing the financial aspects of the domain services provided.

To view the **"Statements"** for your domain, website, or online service, please follow the steps outlined below:

1. Click on the "**Statements"** feature under the **"Accounting**" option, which provides information such as the following:

| Parameter             | Description                                                                                                                                     |
| :-------------------- | :---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Date                  | The date associated with the domain's transactions and activities.                                                                              |
| Reseller Name         | The name assigned to the domain, identifying its ownership or management.                                                                       |
| Reseller Email        | The Reseller Email address used for communication and transactions related to the domain.                                                       |
| Initial Balance (USD) | The starting financial balance or amount associated with the domain, expressed in US dollars.                                                   |
| Final Balance (USD)   | The ending financial balance or amount associated with the domain, expressed in US dollars, after considering all transactions and adjustments. |

| Parameter             | Description                                                                                                                                     |

| Final Balance (USD)   | The ending financial balance or amount associated with the domain, expressed in US dollars, after considering all transactions and adjustments. |
