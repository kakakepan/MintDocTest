Within the **"Accounting"** option, the **"Domain Service Prices"** feature refers to the costs associated with registering and maintaining domain names on behalf of clients. These prices may include registration fees, renewal fees, and any additional services related to domain management. **"Register.domains"** transparently communicates these prices to it's clients, ensuring clarity and understanding regarding the associated costs of domain services.

To view the **"Domain Service Prices"** for your domain, website, or online service, please follow the steps outlined below:

1. Click on the **"Domain Service Prices"** feature under the **"Accounting**" option, which provides information such as the following:

| Parameter  | Description                                                                               |
| :--------- | :---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Extension  | Specifies the domain extension associated with the domain name.                           |
| Service    | The name of the domain service, identifying its purpose or nature.                       |
| Price      | The cost or amount associated with the domain service.                                    |
| Currency   | Indicates the specific currency in which the price for the domain service is denoted.     |
| Min. Years | The minimum number of years for which the domain service order can be placed or is valid. |
