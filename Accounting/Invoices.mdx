Within the **"Accounting"** option, the "**Invoices"** feature is a crucial component of the accounting process, as they are generated based on the service orders and serve as formal requests for payment from clients. These documents provide a detailed breakdown of the specific domain services that have been rendered to the client. **"Invoices"** also specify the payment terms agreed upon between the domain provider and the client, including the due date and acceptable payment methods. By providing a clear summary of the services provided and the associated costs,**"Invoices"** play a vital role in maintaining transparency, ensuring accurate billing, and facilitating timely payments, ultimately contributing to the financial well-being of the customer and **"Register.domains"**

To view the **"Invoices"** for your domain, website, or online service, please follow the steps outlined below:

1. Click on the "**Invoices"** feature under the **"Accounting**" option, which provides information such as the following:

| Parameter      | Description                                                                           |
| :------------- | :------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Date           | The date when the invoice was created.                                                |
| Reseller Email | The email address of the reseller or intermediary involved in the domain transaction. |
| Amount (USD)   | The monetary value in US dollars associated with the domain-related service.          |

   ![1](/images/invoicesimages/1.png)

2. A popup will appear when you select the  **"Search Filters"** option.

   ![2](/images/invoicesimages/2.png)



3. The **"Search Filters"** allow you to specify various parameters which are explained underneath:

| Parameter    | Description                                                                  |
| :----------- | :--------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| ID           | The ID of the domain.                                                        |
| Date         | The date when the invoice was created.                                       |
| Amount (USD) | The monetary value in US dollars associated with the domain-related service. |

   ![3](/images/invoicesimages/3.png)
