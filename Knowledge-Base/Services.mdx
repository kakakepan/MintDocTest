The **"Services"** feature within the **"Knowledge Base"** option explains the **"Domain services"** and **"Hosting services"** by providing a comprehensive understanding of the various essential services involved by explaining their relevant **"Service"**, **"Commands"** and **"Details"**. The **"Services"** feature helps individuals and businesses gain insight into the key services available, their importance, and how they contribute to establishing and maintaining an online presence. It serves as a guide to navigate the **"Register.domains"** landscape, empowering users to make informed decisions and optimize their experience.

# Domain Services

To view the available **"Domain Services"** for your domain, website, or online service, please follow the steps outlined below:

1. Click on the "**Services"** feature under the **"Knowledge Base**" option, which provides information such as the following:

| Parameter            | Description                                                                                                       |
| :------------------- | :---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Service              | Indicates the name of the domain service.                                                                         |
| Triggered By Command | The command or action that initiates a specific event or operation related to the domain services operation.      |
| Service Details      | The specific action or query performed on the domain, providing further information or context about the service. |
