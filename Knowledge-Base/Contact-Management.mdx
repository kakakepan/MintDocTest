Within the **"Knowledge Base"** option, the **"Contact management"** refers to the systematic and organized management of contacts within the **"Register.domains"** ecosystem. It involves handling and maintaining accurate and up-to-date information about individuals or entities associated with a domain, such as domain owners, administrators, technical contacts, and billing contacts. Contact management encompasses tasks like creating and updating contact details, assigning roles and responsibilities, managing contact permissions, and ensuring compliance with relevant regulations and policies. Effective contact management facilitates smooth communication, streamlines administrative processes, and enhances the overall management of domains and related services.
