Within the **"Knowledge Base"** option, the **"Billing"** involves managing financial transactions and invoicing for **"Register.domains"**. It includes calculating costs for registrations, renewals, transfers, and related operations. The billing system ensures accurate pricing, handles payments, generates invoices, and maintains transaction records. It also manages payment methods, discounts, refunds, and credits. Effective billing ensures accurate financial records, seamless payments, and transparent invoicing.
