The SuperReseller dashboard is an online platform or interface designed specifically for SuperResellers. It allows SuperResellers to manage and oversee their reseller operations. With the dashboard, SuperResellers can create and manage reseller accounts, set pricing and product offerings, monitor reseller activities and performance, provision and manage customer accounts, handle billing and invoicing, and access support and communication resources. It provides a centralized control panel for efficient management of reseller business.
